Research and Publications

My research is in algebraic topology, artificial intelligence, and quantum physics.

I currently serve as the Executive Officer (Chair) of the PhD program in Mathematics at the CUNY Graduate Center.

I'm involved with The Initiative for Theoretical Sciences at the CUNY Graduate Center. Check out the ITS calendar to see what's coming up.

Topology Book: Topology: A Categorical Approach is a graduate-level textbook that presents basic topology through the lens of category theory.
